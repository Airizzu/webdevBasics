Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the united states?

Tacos first appeared in the United States in the early 1900s, with the first documented mention of the word appearing in a U.S. newspaper in 1905. They were introduced by Mexican migrants who came to the U.S. to work on the railroads and in the mining industry.
